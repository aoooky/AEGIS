## Detailed Description

**AEGIS** is a next-gen weather extension that goes far beyond basic forecasts. It's a full atmospheric dashboard packed into a sleek dark side panel.

### What's Inside

**Live Weather Data**
Real-time temperature, feels-like, wind speed/direction with animated compass, humidity, pressure, UV index, visibility, dew point, cloud cover, precipitation, snow depth, and soil temperature — all updated every 10 minutes.

**14-Day Forecasts**
Hourly strip for 48 hours, 7-day and 14-day outlooks, daily highs/lows, precipitation probability bars, and a "Best Time for Walk" algorithm that finds the optimal window.

**Severe Weather Alerts**
Automatic detection for thunderstorms, extreme heat/cold, dangerous wind gusts, heavy rain, dense fog, high UV, and frost. Each alert includes color-coded danger levels and actionable advice.

**Air Quality & Pollen**
European AQI breakdown with PM2.5, PM10, Ozone, NO2, SO2 levels. Pollen counts for alder, birch, grass, and ragweed — each with tooltips explaining allergen seasons.

**Storm Risk Score**
Composite 0–100% risk calculation based on CAPE, wind gusts, precipitation, and weather codes. Lists contributing factors in real time.

**Interactive Map**
Leaflet-powered map with dark/light tiles, animated wind stream particles, temperature & pressure overlays, precipitation radar, click-to-forecast, and saved location markers.

**Sun & Moon**
Sunrise/sunset countdown with daylight progress arc, 8-phase moon tracker with illumination percentage, and full sun path visualization.

**Clothing Recommendations**
Smart suggestions based on temperature, precipitation, and conditions — from "heavy coat + scarf" to "sunscreen + hydration."

**Comfort Index**
Heat index, wind chill, and overall comfort rating (Ideal / Comfortable / Acceptable / Uncomfortable).

### Chrome Integration
- **Side Panel** — open with `Ctrl+Shift+E` for a compact always-available view
- **Badge Temperature** — current temp displayed on the extension icon
- **Background Alerts** — Chrome notifications for dangerous conditions (max 3/day)
- **Context Menu** — right-click "Open AEGIS" from anywhere

### Themes & Effects
Three fully styled themes: **Dark** (neon teal), **Light** (green accent), **Biscuit** (warm sepia).

### Offline Support
Full caching layer with automatic fallback. Works offline with last-known data. Online/cached/offline status indicator in footer.

### Data Sources
- Open-Meteo (forecast + air quality + pollen)
- Nominatim / OpenStreetMap (geocoding)
- CARTO (map tiles)

### Privacy
No tracking. No ads. No data collection. All weather data fetched directly from Open-Meteo APIs. Location used only for weather queries.
